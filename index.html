ASRWIS

31 October - 18 November 2016 | Stellenbosch, South Africa

Agnitio Speaker Recognition Workshop in Stellenbosch

ASRWIS is a 3-week research workshop hosted by Agnitio Research at the MIH Media Lab in Stellenbosch, South Africa in November 2016. The workshop follows the tradition of fruitful collaboration summer workshops held in 2008 and 2013 at Johns Hopkins University, in 2010 and 2012 at the Brno University of Technology and in 2015 at Politecnico di Torino.
